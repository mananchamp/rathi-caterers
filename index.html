About Us

A Legacy of Culinary Excellence

Founded in 1968, Rathi Caterers has been a trusted name in the catering industry, serving Mumbai and surrounding areas with dedication and passion. Our journey began with a simple mission: to deliver exceptional food that brings people together.

Today, we continue that legacy by combining traditional recipes with modern culinary techniques, ensuring every dish we prepare tells a story of quality, freshness, and love.
